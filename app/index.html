What Is Clatter?

Clatter is a free and open-source service for workplaces' digital communication! Clatter is made by a small team of developers and a lack of funding, so please, if you can, use our tool to show your support!

I was wondering too... (until I realized I'm a developer for Clatter)

Why Choose Clatter?

These are the reasons of why you should choose Clatter and not other workplace-targetted chatting platforms such as Cliq or Slack:

It's Free

Clatter is free and have no plans currently of changing that, so join us in changing the world of pay walls and overpriced subscriptions!

It's Open Sourced

Clatter is open-sourced and currently also has no plans to change that! Visit Clatter's Repository at www.github.com/Quntem/Clatter.

Regularly Updated

Clatter is regularly updated and is one of the main projects that Quntem, the team behind the development of Clatter, is prioritizing. We see a bright future for Clatter, and we need your support to keep going.
